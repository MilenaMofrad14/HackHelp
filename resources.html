Tips

- Team up. Try to make a team of people with different skills.

- Define a clear problem that you will solve using your project

- Keep it simple, solve one problem at a time

- Do not be afraid to seek feedback and ask for guidance

- Take the opportunity to meet other hackers, mentors, and judges

- Keep a positive attitude, especially when encountering setbacks

- Make your presentation engaging, practice your pitch
